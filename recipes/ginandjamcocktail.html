Gin and Jam Cocktail

This gin and jam cocktail, refreshing and fruity, is a versatile gin cocktail that
is sure to become your new summer favorite. Use any flavor jam you like!

Ingredients

  • 2 fluid ounces gin
  • 1 tablespoon jam, blackberry or any flavor
  • 3/4 fluid ounce fresh lemon juice
  • 1/2 fluid ounce simple syrup
  • 1 cup ice cubes
  • 1 cup crushed ice
  • 1 mint leaf
  • 1 splash club soda (optional)

Steps

  1. Place gin, jam, lemond juice and simple syrup in a cocktail shaker.
  2. Add 1 cup ice cubes.
  3. Seal and shake vigorously until outside is frosted, 10 to 15 seconds.
  4. Fill a glass with crushed ice
  5. Strain cocktail into the glass.
  6. Top with a splash of club soda, if desired
  7. Garnish with mint leaf.
