Spaghetti Carbonara

This bacon carbonara is a super rich, classic "bacon and egg" spaghetti dish
that's great to serve for company. This recipe also makes an unusual brunch offering.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 8 slices bacon, diced
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup dry white wine
  • 4 large eggs, beaten
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • salt and black pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 extra tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook spaghetti in boiling, salted water until tender yet firm to the bite.
  2. Drain, toss spaghetti with 1 tablespoon olive oil, and set aside.
  3. Cook and stir diced bacon in a skillet until evenly browned.
  4. Drain bacon on papel towels, reserving 2 tablespoons bacon fat in the skillet.
  5. Add 1 tablespoon olive oil to bacon fat in the skillet.
  6. Add chopped onion and cook over medium heat until onion is translucent.
  7. Add minced garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  8. Add wine and cook 1 minute more.
  9. Return bacon to the skillet and add spaghetti.
  10. Add beaten eggs, cook and toss until eggs are barely set.
  11. Add 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese, toss again.
  12. Season with salt and pepper.
  13. Serve warm with chopped parsley sprinkled on top and extra Parmesan cheese.
